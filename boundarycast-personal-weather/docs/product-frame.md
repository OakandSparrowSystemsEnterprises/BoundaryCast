# Product Frame

BoundaryCast is your weather forecast, not the area's weather forecast.

It does not present city-level weather as personal truth. It evaluates the forecast for the individual user's lived location, then states what the evidence supports and what it does not support.

Windy remains the gold-standard weather cockpit. BoundaryCast is a governed personal forecast shell for people who need accountable, evidence-bound weather at their exact location.
