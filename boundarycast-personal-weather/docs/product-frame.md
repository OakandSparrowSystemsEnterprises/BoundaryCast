# Product Frame

BoundaryCast is your weather, checked before it speaks.

BoundaryCast travels with you. Wherever you are, it tells you the most specific forecast the evidence supports.

It is your weather forecast, not the area's weather forecast. It does not present city-level weather as personal truth. It evaluates the forecast for the individual user's lived location, then states what the evidence supports and what it does not support.

When exact-location and microclimate evidence is strong, it shows your exact-location forecast. When exact-location evidence is incomplete but area evidence is available, it does not say "no" — it degrades gracefully and shows the responsible general/area forecast with scope disclosure. It never pretends, and it never goes silent unnecessarily.

Windy remains the gold-standard weather cockpit. BoundaryCast is a governed personal forecast shell for people who need accountable, evidence-bound weather at their exact location.
