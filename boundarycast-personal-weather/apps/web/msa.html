1. Service Description

BoundaryCast provides a weather-intelligence platform ("Service") that resolves weather-dependent crowd challenges ("Calls") using governed forecast evidence. The "Call the Weather" feature allows users to stake demonstration credits — not real money — on weather outcomes at specified locations.

2. Demonstration Credits Only

All stakes and payouts within the "Call the Weather" feature are denominated in demonstration credits. No real money, cryptocurrency, or financial instruments are deposited, held, or disbursed. The Service does not constitute a licensed gambling, betting, or financial product.

3. Responsible Participation

The Service is a speculative crowd-challenge feature intended for informational and demonstration purposes. Users should treat participation as entertainment and should not rely on outcomes for financial decisions. Only small, discretionary amounts of demonstration credits should be staked.

4. Location Data

BoundaryCast does not store users' precise location. Location coordinates submitted with a forecast request are used only to generate the governed forecast response for that single request. No location history is retained, logged to persistent storage, or associated with user identity.

5. No Warranty

The Service is provided "as is" for hackathon demonstration purposes. No warranties, express or implied, are made regarding accuracy, availability, or fitness for any particular purpose.

6. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by applicable law. All disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation before any formal proceedings.
